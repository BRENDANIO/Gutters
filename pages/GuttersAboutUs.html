Grushka Gutters

Since 2009, we have proudly been a family owned and operated business, providing exterior finishing services to the interlake, Winnipeg, and the surrounding areas.

Get To Know Our Company

At Grushka Gutters, our goal is to exceed your expectations throughout all aspects of your project – from the first contact to the follow up when the work is completed. Our mindset is to regard your property as though it were our own. Our company’s success is measured by your satisfaction.

With an emphasis on quality, and customer service. we look at each project with the intention of going above and beyond your expectations. By looking at your complete exterior system we can determine what fascia, soffit, eavestrough, gutter guard, and cladding you may need.

At Grushka Gutters, we strive for excellence in everything we do. This philosophy not only extends to the work we do, but also carries over into our customer service and warranty. The three most common compliments we receive from customers are the quality of work, professionalism, and customer service.
